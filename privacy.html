Privacy Policy

Plain-English version. The short answer: we don't collect your stuff.

What we collect

Nothing about you personally. PlayerClip has no user accounts. We don't ask for your name, email, phone number, location, contact list, or any other personal information. There's nothing to sign up for and nothing to log into.

Your videos

PlayerClip needs Photos library access so you can pick videos to tag. Videos you select are processed entirely on your iPhone — they are never uploaded, copied to a server, or shared with us in any way. When you export or share a clip, that happens through Apple's standard share sheet or Photos library, which we do not see or intercept.

In-app purchases

PlayerClip Pro is a one-time in-app purchase handled entirely by Apple's App Store. We never see your payment details — Apple does not share them with us. Apple tells our app whether you've unlocked Pro, and that's the extent of the information exchanged.

Analytics

To understand how PlayerClip is used at an aggregate level, we use TelemetryDeck, a privacy-respecting analytics service. TelemetryDeck does not collect personally identifiable information, does not use the Apple advertising identifier (IDFA), and does not fingerprint devices. The events we record are bucketed and anonymous — for example, "the paywall was shown," not "this user was shown the paywall."

The complete list of events the app records:

  • app.session.start — when the app is opened
  • paywall.shown — when the upgrade screen appears (with which trigger)
  • paywall.dismiss — when the upgrade screen is closed without buying
  • paywall.purchase — when an upgrade purchase completes
  • paywall.restore — when restore-purchases is tapped
  • export.attempted — when an export is started (with format and bucketed clip duration)
  • export.completed — when an export succeeds

You can turn analytics off at any time in Settings → Privacy → Analytics inside the app.

Crash reports

Apple's standard crash reporting may share crash logs with us through Xcode Organizer, but only if you have opted in to "Share with App Developers" in your iPhone's privacy settings. These logs help us fix bugs and contain no personal information.

Children

PlayerClip is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from anyone under 13. If you are under 13, please do not use this app without a parent or guardian.

Third parties

The only third parties involved in PlayerClip are:

  • Apple — for App Store distribution, in-app purchases, crash reporting, and OS-level Photos and share APIs.
  • TelemetryDeck — for the anonymous, bucketed analytics described above.

We do not share, sell, or rent any data to advertisers, data brokers, or any other third party. We have no advertising in the app.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we'll update the date below and (for material changes) post a note on the PlayerClip homepage.

Questions

Questions or concerns about this policy can be raised in your App Store review of PlayerClip — we read every review and respond where appropriate.
