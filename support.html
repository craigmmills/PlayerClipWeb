Support

Help, refunds, and how to report bugs.

Reporting bugs & feedback

The best way to flag a bug or request a feature is to leave a review on the App Store. We read every review and respond there. When reporting an issue, it helps to include:

  • Your iPhone model and iOS version (Settings → General → About)
  • The version of PlayerClip you're running (in-app Settings)
  • A short description of what you were doing when the issue happened

Refunds

PlayerClip Pro is a one-time purchase. Refunds are handled by Apple — request one at reportaproblem.apple.com.

Restoring a purchase

If you've previously bought PlayerClip Pro on this Apple ID, open Settings → Restore Purchases inside the app. Apple handles the rest — there's nothing for us to look up.

Resetting onboarding

To see the first-run onboarding again, delete and reinstall PlayerClip. Your Pro entitlement, if any, will be restored automatically through the App Store.

Privacy

See the Privacy Policy for details on what the app does and does not collect. Short version: nothing about you, no accounts, videos stay on your device.
