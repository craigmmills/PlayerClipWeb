Support

Help, feedback, bug reports — we read every message.

Email

Send us a note at support@playerclip.app. Please include:

  • Your iPhone model and iOS version (Settings → General → About)
  • The version of PlayerClip you're running (Settings → About in the app)
  • A short description of what you were doing when the issue happened

We try to respond within a few days.

Refunds

PlayerClip Pro is a one-time purchase. Refunds are handled by Apple — request a refund at reportaproblem.apple.com.

Resetting onboarding

To see the first-run onboarding again, delete and reinstall the app. (Your purchased Pro entitlement will be restored automatically through the App Store.)
